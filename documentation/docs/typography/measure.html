Measure

Measure or line-length is the distance between the left and right edges of a text block. Overly long lines are a common problem, but they're easy to correct. Shorter lines will make a big difference in the legibility and professionalism of your layout.

Source: Line length | Butterick's Practical Typography

Mnemonics

Base
Modifiers
Optional extensions
measure
-medium — average length of 66 chars
-narrow — average length of 45 chars
-wide — average length of 80 chars

Shorthand

It is possible to use the class name measure instead of measure-medium.

Examples

<p class="measure-narrow" >Lorem ipsum …</p>
